About

PhD in Nanoengineering, specializing in thin-film deposition, interfacial chemistry, and structure– property correlations with extensive experience in atomic layer deposition (ALD), reactive sputtering, pulsed laser deposition and advanced X-ray metrologies. Expert in probing electronic structure, surface hydration, and catalytic behavior using XPS/AP-XPS, XRD, XRR, AFM, Raman, and ellipsometry. Demonstrated ability to independently design experiments, troubleshoot deposition tools, and extract mechanistic insights for electrocatalysis and semiconductor thin-film applications. Passionate about contributing to research in nanofabrication for energy applications and translating materials processing expertise into high-impact scientific discoveries.

About

I am a materials scientist and researcher working at the intersection of nanotechnology, thin-film engineering, and semiconductor processing. My work focuses on the deposition and characterization of functional thin films using Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD) and sputtering.

Research

ALD Ru to RuO₂ Phase Transformation

Study of annealing-driven phase evolution, structure, and surface chemistry.

Reactive Sputtered RuO₂ Thin Films

Effect of Ar:O₂ ratio on stoichiometry, morphology, crystallinity, and electrochemistry.

Web-Based XPS Analysis Tool

Development of a Python-based tool for region selection, background correction, and fitting.
